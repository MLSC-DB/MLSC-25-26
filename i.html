> About MLSC Derabassi

MLSC Derabassi is the official Microsoft Learn Student Chapter of Thapar University, Derabassi Campus — a hub for tech enthusiasts to innovate, collaborate, and grow together in the ever-evolving world of technology.

★ What We Do

  • Hackathons, Gamathons, and Codefests
  • Workshops on web, AI/ML, and dev tools
  • Project-based learning & peer mentorship
  • Interactive community-led events

★ Signature Events

  • Gaming Night – LAN parties, eSports, and pixel chaos
  • Canard – 3 days of Gamathon, Physical Hunt & Mystery Rooms
  • Mystixia – An online cryptic hunt testing digital IQ

★ Why Join?

At MLSC, we believe in learning by doing. Whether you’re into dev, design, gaming, or tech management — this is your launchpad. Join the retro-future of student innovation!
